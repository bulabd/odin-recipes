Olivier salad

A french name, a russian salad:

A very simple dish that is mainly cooked for the celebration of the new year. Simple ingredients and easy preparation, make this salad a one of a kind.

Ingredients (for 8 servings):

  • 2 large boiled potatoes, diced
  • 3 boiled carrots, diced
  • 1 can sweet peas
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon dill weed
  • 3 large sausages, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped

Directions:

  1. Mix everything together to get a homogenous salad.
  2. Enjoy :)
