Blini

Pancakes Russian style:

Almost like basic pancakes but their form is very different. They are flat and thin. It gives the option to put some garnish on it and roll it up like a burrito.

Ingredients (20 blinis):

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/3 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 and 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoon butter or olive oil

Directions:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk eggs, sugar and salt together.
  2. Gradually add milk and flour while whisking until the substance is smooth and well blended.
  3. On medium heat, slowly add a little bit of oil on a frying pan.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of the substance on the pan and fry until light brown crust.
  5. Place them one on top of another and serve.
  6. Enjoy :)
