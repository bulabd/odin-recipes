~Borscht~

A very red soup:

Borscht is a stereotypical Russian dish. It's very popular in Russia and all post-soviet countries due to its simple ingredients and dense flavours. It's main ingredients are potatoes, cabbage and beets which gives this soup a beautiful red color.

Ingredients (for 8 servings):

  • 2 potatoes, quartered
  • 1/2 of a cabbage
  • 3 beets, blended
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 carrots, finely chopped
  • 1 big onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 4 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons frozen or dried dill weed
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Put the tomatoes, carrots, onion, garlic, 3 tablespoons olive oil and a cup of water in a pan and fry for 15min.
  2. Put potatoes, cabbage, beets and the other ingredients in another pot.
  3. Boil on medium heat for 1 and 1/2 hours.
  4. When step 1 is done, add everything from the pan into the pot.
  5. After 1 hour and 1/2, let it rest for 10min.
  6. Enjoy :)
